Overseer is the new standard when it comes to your security. You have a warehouse, a storage facility or property in general that needs protection? Overseer is the efficent solution for your security problem. With the Overseer Watchdrones you do not need several security guards to watch over all of your property. One person is enough to maintain all that is necessary to secure that what is valuable to you. Trust in the never sleeping eyes of our Watchdrones and feel safe at every time. You can order as many drones as you need depending on the size of your property.

Each of our Watchdrones is based on the Parrot AR.Drone 2.0 and commes with a number of additional features.

  • Camera for recording pictures, videos and nav data with instant upload
  • Microphone for hearing everything that goes on in your facility
  • Integrated speakers
  • Floodlights to watch even the darkest corners

To easily controll your drones the AR.Drone node module The NodeCopter is used. This simple tool allows one person to easily access and control all of your drones.
